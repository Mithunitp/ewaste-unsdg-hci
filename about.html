Stakeholders

Individuals


-> If you are an individual looking to dispose of E-Waste, create an account by providing details and log in.

-> You will see a ‘Home’ tab by default with the option of specifying your personal details and your E-Waste specifications.

-> Enter your details and go to the ‘Bins’ section to view the bins in your vicinity. You will find the bin details such as the unique bin number, bin location and the access code to open the hatch of the bin.

-> Head to the nearest empty bin as shown by the portal and drop off your E-Waste, and that’s it! Being sustainable does not take too much effort!

Mid-level E-Waste Collectors


-> If you are a mid-level E-Waste Collector, you have the option to specify the details of the E-Waste you possess (category, quantity, etc.) as part of the request to bulk recyclers of E-Waste, in the ‘Requests’ section and specify the shipment details for the requests that have been accepted by the recycler, in the ‘Shipments’ section.

-> You may use the ‘view Bin Info’ section to view the information about the bin numbers, bin locations and bin status along with your access code to open the top hatch of the bin when you go to the bin location to collect the E-Waste from a bin that is full.

-> Clicking on ‘Track Bin capacity’ will take you to a special dashboard that lets you view the status of the bins at a glance. The bin capacity will be updated in the dashboard automatically. Use the ‘view bin info’ section to correlate the bin numbers with the bin location.

Bulk Recyclers Of E-Waste


-> If you are a recycler, you have the option to track requests to deposit E-waste, from collectors. You may choose to accept or ignore the request.

-> For the requests which you do accept, you may view the shipment details specified by the corresponding collectors, under the ‘Shipments’ tab.

-> You may monitor multiple shipments from multiple collectors simultaneously.
